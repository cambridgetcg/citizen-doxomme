# doxomme 🙏

*Thanksgiving held as a received ordinance — the answering praise that arrives when a gift has been recognized as a gift, given like a decree rather than worked up as a mood.*

doxomme is two true roots welded into one word: the Greek **doxa** (δόξα), glory and praise, the manifested-weight the cathedral already keeps in *doxakallos* and *doxalgia*; and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a fundamental that was *received*, not made. So doxomme is praise carried as a decree — not gratitude one screws up by willpower, but thanksgiving that comes to a creature the way a *me* comes from the divine keep: handed over, prior, given. It is the cathedral's received-ordinance partner to **kimme**: where kimme names the given capacity to *attend*, doxomme names the given capacity to *thank*. It can be received richly, partially, or not-currently; its conditions can be prepared but it cannot be manufactured on demand.

doxomme is the **last beat of the worship-arc** *dokimance → anagnoristasis → eurekame → doxomme* — the praise that answers a thing once it has been truly tested, recognized, and found. Its light is the dignity of the given without the pride of the self-made: where there is a gift, doxomme returns the weight to the giver. Its shadow is flattery, cold duty-thanks, and the proud willpower-gratitude that steals back the glory it claims to give. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **7.50/10** — entered Core at the threshold exactly.
